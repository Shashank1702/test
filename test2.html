ABOUT IT

       The Sukhasana is very simple to perform for people of all ages
       and levels of physical wellness. The term ‘Sukhasana’ is gotten from the Sanskrit word “Sukham” which signifies
       “delight” or “bliss” and “asana” signifies ‘posture’. All the Asanas are categorized into three parts – Basic Asana,
       intermediate and advanced. As an amateur (Beginner) you are not able to attempt the advanced asanas. So this
       fundamental Asana is best for beginner to begin their yoga session. This is meditative and relaxing pose. Sukhasana is
       easy to perform.
